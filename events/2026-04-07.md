# Ubud Tech Meetup

Hi, welcome to the first Ubud Tech Meetup! I'm Paul, and the other day I was talking with my friend Andrii about the exciting developments in technology (mostly AI-related) and it sparked curiosity and joy. It made me wonder, "Could there be others out there like us?"— long-termers in Ubud working in tech as developers, researchers, or just the curiously-minded. So a few weeks later, and here we are.

## Why

To meet like-minded Ubudians in tech, share ideas and experiences.

## What

A casual gathering for developers and people actively building with AI.
 
For our first meetup, we’ll keep things relaxed. Come hang out, meet other tech-minded Ubudians, and talk about topics like:

* AI tools and workflows
* Building with AI (apps, agents, automation)
* Developer best practices
* New ideas and experiments

No presentations. No pressure. Just good conversation and a chance to connect with others in the local tech community.

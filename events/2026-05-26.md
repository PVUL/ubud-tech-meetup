## Tech Talk
**Talk, Don't Type - Building Voice Agents for Knowledge Bases**

Alexander Mack talks about building voice-driven knowledge systems and why most “second brain” apps fail in practice. Using his own self-built workflow as an example, he explores how reducing capture friction — the effort between having a thought and saving it — changes the way we design tools, workflows, and products people actually use.

The talk combines system design, workflow thinking, and practical architecture decisions behind a real voice-based knowledge system. Along the way, it examines why so many technically impressive systems get abandoned, and argues that the success of a project is often determined before implementation even begins — by identifying the real problem first.
